How it's wired

  1. search-data.js lists the pages and sections to search. Edit it to change what's indexed.

  2. seek_locate_display.js is the library. One new SeekLocateDisplay({…}) call builds the box below.

  3. sld.css styles the box; seek_locate_display_highlight.js on each page marks matches on arrival.

Search runs entirely in your browser — no server, no build step. Type above, or tap an example. Click a result to jump straight to that section; press Back and your query and place are restored.
